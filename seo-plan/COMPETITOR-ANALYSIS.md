# Gobiya — Competitor Analysis
*Prepared 2026-07-12. Sources: live web research (Clutch, Semrush Agency Partners, GoodFirms, agency roundups). Directional — validate with a backlink/keyword tool pull before acting on any single number.*

## Competitive sets

Gobiya fights in **two different arenas** with different rivals:

1. **LA local intent** — "seo agency los angeles", "technical seo los angeles", city-level terms
2. **AI-visibility / GEO category** — "generative engine optimization agency", "ai visibility agency", "get cited by chatgpt"

## Arena 1: Los Angeles SEO agencies

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness Gobiya exploits |
|---|---|---|
| **Coalition Technologies** | Named best-for-technical-SEO in 2026 LA roundups; huge case study library; e-commerce depth | Big-agency model — account managers, not principals. Weak GEO story. E-commerce focus leaves local-service/healthcare open. |
| **Bliss Drive** | Since 2007; claims 500%+ traffic outcomes; now sells AI search optimization for ChatGPT/AI Overviews | AI offering reads as bolt-on. Generalist positioning, no founder identity. |
| **ClapCreative** | Aggressive content play (publishes its own "top 10 LA SEO" rankings — and ranks itself #1); LLM-search messaging | Self-ranked lists undercut trust; a transparency/proof angle beats this. |
| **LA SEO (laseo.com type players)** | Volume link building (claims 100k+ backlinks built), in-house outreach | Quantity-over-quality link story is increasingly a liability; positions Gobiya's "editorial links only" cleanly. |
| **Agency Jet / Boostability** | Strong Clutch review profiles (90–100% satisfaction themes); process transparency | National/productized — no LA depth, no AI-citation specialism. |

**Takeaway:** every LA rival is either big-and-generalist or self-promotional. None owns "founder-operated + AI-visibility." The head term "seo agency los angeles" is a 12-month fight; "technical seo los angeles" + vertical/city long-tail is winnable in 3–6.

## Arena 2: GEO / AI-visibility specialists (national)

| Competitor | Strength | Relevance to Gobiya |
|---|---|---|
| **Go Fish Digital** | Proprietary tools (AI Overview Analyzer, semantic content audits); strong educational content | The content-depth benchmark. Their service page structure is worth studying, not copying. |
| **Directive** | B2B GEO positioning, "AI-ready content architecture" methodology | Competes only for Gobiya's enterprise-b2b vertical, not local. |
| **NoGood** | Goodie platform for real-time LLM visibility monitoring | Tooling story. Gobiya counters with SE Ranking AI SoV reporting at a fraction of cost. |
| **Stratabeat** | Deep technical GEO content (cosine similarity, multimodal optimization) | Sets the bar for what "credible GEO expertise" content looks like. |
| **Power Digital / Intero Digital** | Case studies with big numbers (e.g. 5,665% generative-visibility growth) | Proof that citable GEO metrics win the category — reinforces the case-study priority. |

**Market pricing signal:** GEO is being sold at ~$750/mo as an add-on and from ~$1,750/mo combined with SEO. Buyer-beware sentiment is high ("everyone added GEO to the pitch deck") — proof beats claims.
